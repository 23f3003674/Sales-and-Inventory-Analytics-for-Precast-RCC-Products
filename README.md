📊 Sales and Inventory Analytics for Precast RCC Products

📌 About the Project

This project is about a business that sells precast RCC products. I looked at sales, inventory and customer payment data to see how the business is doing and where it can improve.

The main goal was to use data to make decisions about inventory planning and credit management. These are two challenges for the business.

🎯 What I Tried to Solve

I tried to solve the problem of unpredictable demand will be which makes it hard to plan inventory.

I tried to solve the problem of customers paying late and having a lot of outstanding payments.

📂 Data Used

I used data that came directly from the business. This data included sales and inventory information for 10 products.

It also included customer payment data for 29 clients, including payments and outstanding dues.

⚙️ What I Did

🔹 Demand Forecasting

I used a tool in Excel to estimate what demand will be in the future and to see if there are any trends.

🔹 ABC Analysis

I put products into three groups based on how they contribute to sales. This helped me see which products are the important.

🔹 Customer Risk Analysis

I looked at customers. Used financial measures to see how risky they are.

I used things like how it takes them to pay what percentage of their payments are outstanding and how efficient they are at paying.

Then I put customers into groups based on how risky they are.

📊 Key Observations

I found out that a few products make up most of the sales.

I also found out that demand is not the same every month it changes.

I found out that a lot of customers pay late or have a lot of outstanding payments.

This is affecting the businesss cash flow.

💡 What I Suggested

Inventory and Demand

The business should focus more on high-value products.

It should use demand forecasting to plan what to buy and when.

Payments

The business should be stricter, with customers who're high-risk.

It should try to get payments before taking new orders.

🛠 Tools Used

I used Microsoft Excel to clean and analyze the data.

I also used some data visualization techniques.

📈 Outcome

This project helps the business manage its inventory better.

It helps reduce stock and costs.

It helps improve cash flow by controlling payment delays.
